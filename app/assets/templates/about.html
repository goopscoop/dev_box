Inception

DevBox is probably the best place on the internet. It's so great because it's a place where you can finally, easily find the development tools you want to use on your next project. Or, find the tools you should have used on your last one (which is always good to know, but kinda sucks when you find out).

Developed by Nick Cronquist and S. Cody Barrus in the last week of June 2015 (only 6 days from conception to production), we wanted a place to organize all of our dev tools, repos and docs in a way that works better than just the bookmarks toolbar. So we made it.

Technologies

DevBox is powered by Rails, Angular.js, and Postgres, styled with Materialize.css and Material, and fueled by late night caffine enimas and early morning boozings.
